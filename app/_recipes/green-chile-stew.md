ingredients:
- 1 jar of [green chile stew mix](http://amzn.to/1KYXSjo)
- 1lb ground chicken
- 1 red onion
- 1 green onion
- 1 can corn

directions:
- Start onions in pan, cook down
- Add chicken, corn
- Add stew mix
- Serve with croutons
