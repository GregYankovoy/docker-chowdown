ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 2 tsp vanilla 
- sprinkle of sugar
- 4 cups Nilla Wafers Minis

directions:
- Beat the eggs, heavy cream, vanilla, and sugar.
- Arrange the nillas in a few flat layers in a baking dish.
- Pour egg mixture over nillas. Let soak while oven preheats to 350Â°. Top with shake of cinnamon sugar.
- Bake until egg has set (it'll fluff up a bit)
